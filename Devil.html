Loki prince of Asgard

God of Mischief and brother to Thor, Loki's tricks and schemes wreak havoc across the realms. Loki, Prince of Asgard, Odinson, rightful heir of Jotunheim, and God of Mischief, is burdened with glorious purpose. His desire to be a king drives him to sow chaos in Asgard.Loki's personality in The Avengers is sensitive, intelligent, and introverted. In combination with his poor self-image, these qualities can make Loki spiteful, jealous, and angry. Loki is shrewd and thoughtful, though, especially when compared to Thor. He has a strategic mind, capable of being conniving and sly.Everything he does is a result of his childhood always overshadowed by Thor never really appreciated and he should have been king of Jotunheim.

Narrative photography

In 2012, Loki attacks a remote S.H.I.E.L.D. research facility, using a scepter that controls people's minds and, amplifies his hatred for Thor and inhabitants of Earth

He uses the scepter to brainwash Clint Barton and Dr.Erik Selvig and steals the Tesseract. In Stuttgart, Barton steals iridium to stabilize the Tesseract's power.

Loki causes a distraction, leading to a brief confrontation with Steve Rogers, Tony Stark, and Natasha Romanoff that ends with Loki allowing himself to get captured.

Loki is imprisoned while Bruce Banner and Stark attempt to locate the Tesseract. Agents possessed by Loki attack the Helicarrier, disabling one of its engines.

Loki kills the agent Phil Coulson and ejects Thor from the Helicarrier and escape in the plane brought by his mens, to execute his plan to destroy earth.

Loki use Tesseract with a device Selvig built, to open a wormhole above Stark Tower in New York City to the Chitauri fleet in space, launching his invasion.

The Avengers arrive and rally in defense of the city. As the Chitauri are ultimately defeated, the Hulk attacks Loki and beats him into submission in the Tower.

Loki is arrested by Avenger team and his plan was abandoned. Thor take loki along with Tesseract Cube to Asgard.
