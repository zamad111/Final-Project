Nick Fury Biography

Nicholas Joseph Fury was born in Huntsville, Alabama on 4 July 1950. He joined the United States Army after graduating from high school, eventually becoming a Colonel. He later left the army to join the Central Intelligence Agency, where he served as a spy during the Cold War.
